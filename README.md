# BasuDevGames
## Beware of Bouncy Ball

It's the battle of the shapes. You get to play Mr. Rect, he is handsome and wants to be the best. On the other corner, we have Mr. Bouncy, the bounciest shape on Earth. Mr. Rect has to beat Mr. Bouncy to become the greatest shape of all time. If Mr. Bouncy catches Mr. Rect, that will be the end for Mr. Rect. Every time Mr. Bouncy hits the wall, he becomes quicker, but each time he hits a wall, Mr. Rect's score increases. Mr. Rect has to be vigilant and collect the energy packages, which are offered to him after every 6 points that he makes Mr. Bouncy wait. Lets see how long Mr. Rect can survive in it..
